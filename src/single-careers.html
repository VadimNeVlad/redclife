Paralegal to Junior Associate in Banking and Finance practice

Redcliffe Partners is looking for a Paralegal / Junior Associate to join its Banking & Finance practice.

If you want to join our young and dynamic team and contribute to our work on high-profile, innovative and challenging international transactions, we encourage you to apply.

Main requirements:

  • A law degree from a reputable Ukrainian law school. A recognised LL.M. degree would be an advantage
  • Previous work experience would be an advantage, but not a must
  • Fluent English, Ukrainian and Russian
  • Excellent analytical, legal writing and attention to detail skills

We offer:

  • A supportive team and a friendly working environment
  • Exciting experience in unique projects
  • Market-leading remuneration
  • A targeted, innovative learning and working experience, as well as professional and personal growth opportunities.

Please submit your resume and a cover letter indicating your desired position to office@redcliffe-partners.com.
